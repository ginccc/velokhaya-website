LIFE CYCLING

We don't just teach cycling—we cycle lives forward. Sport is the carrot. Education is the purpose. Nutrition is the fuel.

The Peloton Effect

We Are The Peloton For Khayelitsha's Youth

In cycling, the peloton is the pack. Riders save up to 40% of their energy by drafting behind those in front—the strong shield the vulnerable from the wind.

Velokhaya is that peloton for Khayelitsha's youth. We shield them from the headwinds of township life—crime, hunger, and broken schools—so they can save their energy for the moments that matter: exams, races, and life's finish lines.

The Carrot

From Shack to Podium

The bicycle represents freedom, status, and adrenaline. It attracts youth who might otherwise be drawn to gang culture. We use this "carrot" to drive discipline and excellence.

The Purpose

No Homework, No Ride.

Access to the bikes is strictly conditional. If a child does not attend the learning lab or complete their schoolwork, they are denied access to the sport. This transforms the bicycle into a lever for educational engagement.

Academic Mentorship

Partnerships with "The Answer Series" and "Infinite Family" provide study guides and virtual mentoring to ensure our riders succeed off the bike.

Alumni Success: Xolisa Makubalo

From rider to volunteer coach to UWC Sports Science graduate. Xolisa embodies the full "Life Cycling" journey.

The Fuel

You Can't Train a Hungry Child

Food insecurity is a primary barrier to both physical training and cognitive development. In partnership with the Peninsula School Feeding Association (PSFA), we ensure our riders have the caloric surplus necessary to win.

PSFA Partner

Daily Meals

Nutritious breakfast and lunch menus served every day to fuel training and learning.

Dignity First

We don't run a soup kitchen line. We provide a family dining environment with proper crockery and cutlery to ensure dignity.

Community Powered

Meals are prepared by community volunteers—mothers and grandmothers—reinforcing local ownership.

Civic Duty

Beyond the Peloton: Safe Cycling Advocacy

We extend our impact beyond our own members by advocating for safer roads for all South Africans. Through our "Look to Save Lives" campaign, we fight to protect every cyclist on the road.
