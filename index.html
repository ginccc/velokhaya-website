THE PELOTON EFFECT

In cycling, the peloton moves as one. The strong protect the weak. They share the wind. They move faster together than they ever could alone.

At Velokhaya, we are that peloton for the youth of Khayelitsha. We provide the slipstream that allows young riders to save energy for the moments that matter—school, life, and the finish line.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How can I join Velokhaya?
Any young person from Khayelitsha who loves cycling and is serious about school can join the family. Come to the academy at Khayelitsha Stadium during operating hours, meet our coaches, and sign up. No bike? No problem—we'll sort you out.
Do you accept bike donations?
Absolutely! Your old bike gathering dust could become someone's ticket to the world stage. We accept bicycles, cycling gear, and components. Contact us to arrange a drop-off or collection—your pre-loved kit can change a life.
How can I support the academy financially?
You can support us by making a one-time donation or becoming a monthly donor. Visit our Support page for banking details and other ways to give.
Where is Velokhaya located?
We are based at the Khayelitsha Stadium, Phendula Crescent, Khayelitsha, Cape Town. We operate in the heart of the community we serve.
