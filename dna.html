OUR DNA

Born in a shipping container. Raised on the road. Driven by a belief that every child deserves a shot at the podium.

The Origin Story

Velokhaya began in 2003 with a radical idea: give a township child a bicycle, and you give them more than transport—you give them freedom, discipline, and a reason to dream bigger.

Starting from a small container in Khayelitsha, we have grown into a world-class cycling academy. We don't just train cyclists; we develop leaders who understand that the steepest hills lead to the best views. Today, we are transforming from that shipping container into a high-performance centre.

"Cycling is just the carrot: education is the purpose."

— Sipho Mona, GM

"We are not just a cycling club. We are a family."
